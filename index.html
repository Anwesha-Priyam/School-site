campus life

School Canteen
The school canteen is big and many people can sit on one bench. They give different food items to eat. They are very tasty. It tastes just like how my mom makes.
School Playground
The school playground is big. We get to play many games like badminton and football. It has one staircase for those who are feeling tired after a game and one sandpit to build with sand. Usally, they don't let us play in the sandpit as it will go into peoples eyes and dirty their clothes. People can stay in the class if they don't want to play at the first place. We also get awards or do the sports on the playground.
